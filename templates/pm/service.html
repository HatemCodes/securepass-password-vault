Our Service

SecurePass is a lightweight, privacy-focused password manager that lets users safely encrypt and manage their sensitive information. Whether you're storing passwords, generating strong credentials, or experimenting with RSA encryption.

🔒 Local Encryption

All encryption and decryption are done in your browser. We use RSA (public/private key) encryption for secure handling of your data.

🧠 User-Friendly

The interface is clean, intuitive, and built with usability in mind — no clutter, just simplicity and speed.
